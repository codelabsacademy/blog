Ever wonder why you keep seeing those phishing scams, even after all the training? You're not alone! A recent report shows that employee clicks on phishing websites almost tripled last year.

The Sneaky New Phishing Tactics

It's not just emails anymore. Attackers are using search engines and hiding links in ads and comments on popular websites. They're even targeting cloud services like Microsoft and Adobe, aiming for your company's sensitive data.

Why Are People Still Clicking?

It's getting harder to spot fake websites. Plus, with so many phishing attempts, people get tired and less vigilant. It's like trying to spot a tiny detail in a huge, busy picture.

The Shadow IT Problem

Using personal cloud apps for work is risky. It's easy to accidentally share company data, especially with automatic phone backups. Think of it like leaving your keys in a public place.

AI and Cybersecurity

Generative AI tools are everywhere, but they also pose a risk. Many companies are blocking certain AI apps to prevent data leaks. It's a balancing act between using helpful tools and protecting your information.

What Can We Do?

Companies are using data loss prevention and coaching tools to help. It's a team effort to stay safe online. We all need to be more aware and careful.

Learn more about protecting yourself and your company from cyber threats. There are many resources available to help you stay safe online.
