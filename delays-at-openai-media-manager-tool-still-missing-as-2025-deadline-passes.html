Ever wonder how AI companies use your work? OpenAI promised a tool to let creators control their data by 2025. It's late.

The Delay

OpenAI's Media Manager tool, meant to give creators more say over how their work is used in AI training, is behind schedule. Insiders say it wasn't a priority. That's a problem.

The Problem

AI models learn from tons of data. This can lead to copyright issues if they copy stuff without permission. OpenAI's tool was supposed to help fix this. But it's not ready.

The Solution?

While there are some workarounds, they aren't ideal. Experts aren't sure if the Media Manager will even solve everything. It might just be for show.

The Future

OpenAI faces lawsuits. This tool is a way to show they care about creators' rights. But will it be enough? Only time will tell. Meanwhile, learn more about AI and data with Code Labs Academy's Data Science & AI Bootcamp!
